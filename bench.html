Many people find comfort in creating a memorial to a loved one following a burial or cremation. Memorials can provide a peaceful place to visit and remember those who have passed away.

These plaques are designed to be attached to the back rest of benches in one of two locations. They provide a touching memorial and somewhere you can sit and remember your loved one.

Ordering a plaque is simple - just follow the steps below.

1) Choose your text

Write a maximum of six lines with up to 50 characters per line.

2) Choose an image

If you want an image, choose one from the gallery below. You will be able to select this image on the online form.

3) Choose your location

Bench plaques can be located at either Southampton Crematorium or South Stoneham Garden of Rest.

4) Choose the length of dedication

Plaques can be dedicated for 5, 10 or 20 years. After this time they can be renewed.

Dedications for 5 or 10 years can be applied for online.

For a 20 year dedication you will need to complete the downloadable form below.

5) Make sure you have all your details to hand

The online form will take approximately 15 minutes to complete. Ensure you have all necessary information to hand as there is no facility to save the form part way through.

As payment needs to be taken before the form can be submitted, ensure you have your credit/debit card to hand. Your card must be 3D Secure for payment to be successful.

6) Fill in the form

The online form allows you to design your plaque, creating an image of how it will look with the text and any images you select.

Once we receive your order, a proof of your memorial will be created and sent to you for sign off. If you are happy we will produce and install your memorial, advising you when it is ready to view.

The time scale for delivery is approximately two months.

* - 20-year period forms should be completed and emailed to bereavement.services@southampton.gov.uk

Design details

  • DIMENSIONS - 6in x 2in (150mm x 50mm)
  • MATERIALS - Gold-coloured aluminium with engraved text and graphics
  • TEXT - Can accommodate a maximum of six lines with up to 50 characters per line. All text is Times New Roman font – font size depends on amount of text. Names will appear on the second line in uppercase, bold font. Verses or quotations can be in italics.
  • DECORATION - A plain border can be added. Images (see the gallery on this page) can be added to customise the plaque

Locations

  • Southampton Crematorium
  • South Stoneham Garden of Rest

We can cater for a number of memorial plaques to be fixed to each bench.

Cost

Cost is subject to final design and will be shown on the online form.

The cost for a 20-year dedication plaque with the basic design is £511.81 including VAT.
